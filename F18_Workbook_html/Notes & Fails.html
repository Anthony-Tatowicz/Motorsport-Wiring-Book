Devices Controlled w/ ECU Digital Output
26
Counter-intuitively, the ECU's "digital output" pins do not source current or provide voltage, they are simply low-side switches inside the ECU. ECU-controlled relays should have their coils on the high-side of the circuit. DO NOT CONNECT HIGH-CURRENT DEVICES DIRECTLY THROUGH THE ECU!
